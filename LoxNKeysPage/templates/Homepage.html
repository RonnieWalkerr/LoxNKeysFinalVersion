About

The HAN BioCentre is preforming a research on the lipoxygenase enzyme (LOX).
There are a lot of papers writen on this subject and reading them would take ages.
So the BioCentre wants an application made to analyse all these papers and have a search tool to show only the relevant papers.
We are a group of Bioinformatics students studying at the HAN in Nijmegen.
